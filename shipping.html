Shipping and Delivery Policy

This policy defines how physical and service deliveries are handled by Reydium Digital.

Physical Shipment Guidance

For international buyers, shipments are handled through registered international courier companies and/or international speed post. For domestic buyers, shipments are handled through registered domestic courier companies and/or speed post.

  • Dispatch timelines are shared at order confirmation.
  • Delivery timelines depend on courier and postal service norms.
  • Address supplied by the buyer is treated as the final delivery address.

Service Delivery Guidance

For digital services, delivery is typically confirmed via registered email communication and project updates. Service start dates and milestones are agreed during engagement kickoff.

  • Support Desk: support@reydium.in
  • Help Contact: +91 88600 14150
  • Escalation: Reach out through the same channels with order or project details.

Liability and Delays

Reydium Digital is not liable for delays caused by courier companies or postal authorities. Our responsibility is limited to handing over consignments within committed dispatch windows communicated at order confirmation.
